Who We Are

About Solista Energies

Solista Energies Pvt. Ltd. is a leading solar EPC company delivering end-to-end and turnkey renewable energy solutions for Commercial, Industrial, and Utility Scale Projects across India. We are committed to accelerating India's transition towards clean and sustainable energy by delivering high-quality solar infrastructure.

Established with a vision to make solar power accessible, reliable, and cost-effective, we provide complete engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) services, along with customized system design, flawless installation, commissioning, and operation & maintenance (O&M). Our experienced team ensures every project is executed with precision, safety, and adherence to industry standards.
